Frequently Asked Questions

United Hopes is a donation platform that connects one donor to one recipient, ensuring personal and impactful contributions.

When you choose a recipient to donate to, you can directly send your contribution. The recipient gets notified, and you can track the progress of your donation.

Yes, we use encryption and secure servers to protect your personal information and ensure that your data is never shared with unauthorized parties.

Currently, we do not offer recurring donation options. However, you can make one-time donations to multiple recipients at any time.

If a recipient no longer requires assistance, they can opt-out of receiving donations, and the funds will be returned to the donor. You will be notified of any changes.

You can contact us via email at contactunitedhope@gmail.com or reach out to our support team through the Contact page on our website.

Once your donation is made, you will receive updates from the recipient through the platform. You can also monitor their progress and see how your donation is being used in real-time.

Currently, United Hopes does not issue tax receipts for donations, as we do not function as a registered charity. Please consult with your tax advisor for further guidance.
